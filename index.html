Incomplete

This is incomplete section is for story that will be completed by the Author, Hiatus/Discontinued story have another section. It can be incomplete because various reasons including various author's reasons or various translations reasons or various reader reasons. The reasons included: The translator keep changing and go back to chapter 1, the story is still on going, the author is resting, reader resting from the story (because it is a heavy story), or the reader haven't continue reading the story.
